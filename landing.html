What's Inside

🥁

19 Sample Pads

14 built-in sounds synthesized via Web Audio API — kicks, snares, hats, claps, toms, synths, bells, vinyl crackle, sub boom, and FX glitch.

🎛️

16-Step Sequencer

Program beats step by step with ghost/normal/accent velocity, swing control up to 75%, and record-in-place by tapping pads live.

📦

4 Sample Packs

Switch between Factory, LoFi, 808, and Electro packs on the fly. Each pack has unique drum sounds and synth tones.

♫

12 Preset Beats

Boom Bap, Trap 808, House, Techno, D&B, Lo-Fi, Disco, Reggaeton, Bossa, Ambient, Afrobeat — load instantly with one click.

🌙

Night Mode

Toggle between vintage cream light mode and a slick dark theme with cyan LCD accents and magenta keys.

🎹

MIDI + Keyboard

Full physical keyboard mapping plus automatic MIDI controller detection. Plug in your Akai or Novation and play.

📊

Live Waveform LCD

Real-time AnalyserNode waveform on the LCD display. Press F2 to toggle between ASCII and canvas oscilloscope mode.

🎵

WAV Export

Render your beat to a WAV file using OfflineAudioContext. Download and use in your DAW.

💾

Auto-Save Patterns

Patterns persist to localStorage automatically. Share via JSON, clipboard, download, or URL share links.
